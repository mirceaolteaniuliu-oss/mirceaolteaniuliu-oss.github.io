The Delivery Health Diagnostic

20 minutes. No pitch. A clear framing of the problem and at least one thing you can act on immediately.

What it is

Most transformation conversations start in the wrong place — with solutions. Before I recommend anything, I want to understand the specific shape of your problem.

The Delivery Health Diagnostic is a structured 20-minute conversation focused on three questions: Where is change stalling in your organisation? What has already been tried? And what would "fixed" actually look like for your teams?

What you get

You'll leave with a clear framing of the problem — not a diagnosis you could have Googled, but a specific read on where the friction lives in your organisation. And at least one thing you can act on immediately, regardless of whether we work together.

There's no deck. No follow-up proposal unless you ask for one. If there's a fit, we'll both know by the end of the call.
